Latest News About NewJeans

NewJeans is a South Korean K-pop girl group that debuted in July 2022 under the label ADOR, which is part of HYBE. The group quickly became one of the most influential 4th-generation K-pop acts thanks to their fresh concept, nostalgic sound, and strong international popularity. The group was created by producer and creative director Min Hee‑jin, who wanted to introduce a style of K-pop that felt more natural and youthful compared with many heavily stylized idol groups.

Danielle Officially Removed from NewJeans After Contract Termination.
06 minute read 3.5k Views 05 Comment 1.5k Share

Danielle Marsh is no longer a member of NewJeans after her contract with ADOR was terminated in December 2025. The decision followed a long legal and management dispute involving ADOR, HYBE, and former ADOR CEO Min Hee-jin. As a result, NewJeans continues activities without Danielle, while legal conflicts connected to the case are still ongoing.
